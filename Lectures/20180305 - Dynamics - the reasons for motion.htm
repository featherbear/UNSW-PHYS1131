Dynamics - the reasons for motion

Newton's Laws of Physics

  1. Every body persists in its state of rest or of uniform motion in a straight line, until it is compelled to change that state by forces impressed on it.


 

 

  1. In inertial frames
     
  2.  
    1. For every action there is always an opposing equal action
    2. The mutual actions of two bodies upon each other are always equal and directed to each other
    3. Forces always occur in symmetrical pairs
    4. Forces come in symmetric pairs that add to zero
       

Note - The total internal forces in a system is zero.
